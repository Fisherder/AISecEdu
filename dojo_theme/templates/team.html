About AISecEdu.

AISecEdu is a hands-on cybersecurity course platform. It organizes learning as courses → units → exercises and keeps instructions, an isolated workspace, guided hints, submissions, and progress in one continuous workflow.

For students

Choose a course, work through its units, and launch each exercise directly into Terminal, Code, or Desktop mode. Progress and accepted submissions are recorded at the course and unit levels.

For teachers

Create versioned courses, add units and exercises, review class progress, and use evidence-backed learning analytics without interrupting the practical workspace.

Learning principles

  • Practice in clean, reproducible exercise environments.
  • Keep navigation and progress visible at every level.
  • Offer guided questions and next-step prompts without exposing complete solutions.
  • Make course content reviewable, reusable, and straightforward to maintain.
